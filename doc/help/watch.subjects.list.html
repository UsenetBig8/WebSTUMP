Untrusted subjects list is the list of subjects which should always be reviewed by mods, even if the messages are posted by preapproved posters.

Example:

abortion
gun control
forger
The above would ensure that all discussions with Subject: containing one of the above words would be monitored. A nice way to deal with controversial topics or flames.
